Introducing The All New Pairs Matching Film Trivia Quiz Game Challenge!

What is it?

Great question. This isn't your average pairs game, oh no no. Here you'll have you use every bit of your memory based pair matching skills, but you'll also need to call upon your film based trivia knowledge.


How to Play

All you have to do it match the film quote to the film. Easy. Click on any of the cards to reveal the quote or image behind it. Then try to find the matching film or quote. The game is timed and you'll receive bonus points for how quickly you complete the game. Be careful though, you'll also lose marks depending on how many attempts you take.
